Tribute app

The Tribute App, designed with utmost respect, commemorates Nepal's four martyrs which is celebrated every year in Nepal on Magh 16 as a Mayter's Day. Dedicated to Shukraraj Shastri, Dharma Bhakta Mathema, Dashrath Chand, and Ganga Lal Shrestha, this digital tribute preserves their enduring legacy and the historic significance of their sacrifice. May it serve as a reminder of their contributions and inspire gratitude for these national heroes.
